Literature Review

In academia, people at times encounter research happening in various different frontiers. Specialists of similar fields, working at their own respective frontiers, are able to communicate with surprising ease and efficiency. Naturally, knowing and understanding complicated words and their scientific or mathematical meaning is important. But it is commonplace, as a newcomer in a research domain, to be lost in the jargon. I begin the documentation to give the motivation and background in a simple and friendly manner.
